What actually happened?

To prepare for the collapse of society, some people started stocking up on food, water, and basic necessities as some thought Y2K was going to be a technological apocalypse.

In order to prevent the supposed shut down of all technological systems, President Bill Clinton established the Council on Year 2000 Conversion. The U.S. government spent over $250 billion to solve the problem. After the fact, he called Y2K “the first challenge of the 21st century successfully met”.
