Governments created task forces and worked with the private sector and computer programmers to fix the problem and prepare their system so that they would continue to be able to work after December 31, 1999.

In order to be prepared, companies and businesses across the globe shut down their operations on New Years Eve, took extra cyber security precautions, and some banks took extra money in cash.

But when the clock struck midnight, barely anything happened. Some ATMs stopped working or certain signs displayed the wrong date, however nothing impacted the world as much as some people thought it would.
